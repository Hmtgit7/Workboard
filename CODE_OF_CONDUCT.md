# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We respect people of all backgrounds and identities. We are committed to creating a community that is free of harassment and discrimination.

## Expected Behavior

**Be respectful.** Disagreements are inevitable, but disagreeing respectfully is important. Value differing viewpoints and opinions.

**Be direct but professional.** Comments should be constructive and relevant.

**Be inclusive.** Seek diverse perspectives. Diversity of thought and background strengthens our work.

**Protect confidentiality.** Anything said in private channels should remain confidential unless given permission to share.

**Be patient.** Remember that everyone is volunteering their time. Appreciate the work done, and try to help when others need it.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Profanity, insults, or personal attacks
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Posting private information without consent
- Spam or off-topic content
- Trolling, flaming, or other disruptive behavior

## Consequences

Community members who violate this code of conduct may be subject to:

- Warning from moderators
- Temporary ban from the community
- Permanent ban from the community
- Other actions deemed appropriate

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to: conduct@example.com

Include:

- Description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When it occurred
- Any relevant context

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
